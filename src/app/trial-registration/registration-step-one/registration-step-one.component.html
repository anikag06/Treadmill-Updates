Are you feeling low, down, or depressed?
TreadWill is a website designed to help individuals deal with stress, low mood, lethargy, and other depressive symptoms. We are conducting a study to see if TreadWill is effective in helping people deal with these symptoms.
Why you might want to join :
Learn skills to deal with stressful situations.
Contribute to improving mental health care.
Participation is free, anonymous and does not require talking to anyone.
Requires just 15 minutes of daily learning over a period of six weeks, from the privacy of your room.
