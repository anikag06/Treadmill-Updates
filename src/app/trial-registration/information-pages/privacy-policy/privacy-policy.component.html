Introduction

Who does this policy apply to?

This privacy policy applies to all previous, present, and future TreadWill users.

What is collected?

We collect:
the minimal personal information - including, but not limited to, email address, phone number, age, gender, educational qualification - required to provide personalized services, enhance the user experience, and for research purposes; any information you submit when using the TreadWill website (including forms, feedbacks, notes, posts, comments, likes, questionnaires, mood ratings, quizzes, and conversation histories);

We don't, however, require you to disclose your name at any point during your usage of TreadWill. We don't assume any responsibility if you, on your own volition, post your identity within the TreadWill website or provide information that identifies you definitively.

How do we collect and hold personal information?

We collect information from you when you provide the information via the TreadWill websites. All communication is encrypted and transmitted via SSL which is an industry standard information transfer protocol. However, no method of electronic transmission or method of data storage is perfect or impenetrable, so we cannot guarantee that your information will be absolutely safe from intrusion during transmission or storage. TreadWill website is hosted on third-party servers.

Who do we disclose your personal information to?

The primary purpose of collecting data from users is for improving our service and for research, and as such, your unidentified data will be available to TreadWill team and our collaborators.

We may disclose your personal information: when we are required to do so by law;
when we feel doing so may protect you or other users from harm;
to entities who assist us in providing our services (including hosting providers).

Also, our research is likely to lead to scientific publications. In such publications, pooled data will be published. The published data, however, can't be used to identify any individual user.
