How to interpret the graph?
x axis: Module Number

y axis: GAD-7 score at the beginning of that module. GAD-7 score indicates the severity of your depressive symptoms. In the general population, the average score is typically around 3 although it tends to vary person to person. A score of 20 or more usually indicates severe depression.

A decreasing trend in the graph means that you are doing an excellent job in dealing with your depressive symptoms.
An increasing trend in the graph means that there is room for improvement in dealing with your depressive symptoms.
