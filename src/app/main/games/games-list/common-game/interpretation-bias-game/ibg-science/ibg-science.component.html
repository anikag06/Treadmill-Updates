Science behind the game

Research shows that someone undergoing an episode of depression often jump to a negative conclusion. Given an ambiguous situation, they think that the worst outcome is going to happen. This game has been designed to train your mind to link positive outcomes to ambiguous situations.
For this game to work, you have to play this game repeatedly. You can't just do it once or twice and leave it. Play this game every week so that you earn at least one gold badge every week.
